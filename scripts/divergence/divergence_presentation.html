Divergence Analysis
Team Meeting Brief

How electoral and civil liberties sub-indices diverge in Colombia

Based on the June 2026 divergence memo. This deck centers the evidence visually and highlights the findings, spatial pattern, and recommendations for the paper.

Prepared for: SN V-Dem team meeting
Location: scripts/divergence

Research question

  • Does the snelect / sncivlib divergence analysis belong in the paper?
  • What theoretical work does it do?
  • Can divergence be meaningful even when the composite is valid?

Key conceptual point

  • High sub-index correlation is expected and reflects valid shared structure.
  • Divergence asks what is left when similar dimensions split.
  • Moran’s I = 0.51, p < 2.2e-16: the pattern is spatial, not random.

Theoretical patterns

  • Paramilitary democracy: elections yes, civil liberties no.
  • Guerrilla quasi-states: both dimensions remain low.
  • Magdalena Medio / Santander vs. Pacific Coast is the expected split.

Temporal evidence

  • Post-FARC shows the strongest elections-dominant divergence.
  • AUC demobilization shows more convergence.
  • The timing supports theory about armed actor transitions.

Quantitative results

  • Correlation: r = 0.958
  • Mean normalized divergence: +0.045 (elections lead)
  • Mean raw divergence: −0.014
  • Spatial clustering: Moran’s I = 0.51

Interpretation

  • Use divergence as suggestive evidence, not causal proof.
  • Frame it as a construct validity diagnostic.
  • The pattern fits theory about different armed actor regimes.

Recommendation for the paper

Include divergence briefly as a diagnostic, not as a central finding:

  • Support the validity of electoral and civil liberties sub-indices.
  • Emphasize theory-driven pattern: paramilitary democracy vs. guerrilla convergence.
  • Note shared predictors and expert co-movement limit the detectable magnitude.
Next step: add a short subsection in the paper introducing divergence as a confirmatory diagnostic.

Takeaway

High sub-index correlation is expected. Divergence reveals meaningful corridor-level variation that a composite would obscure.

  • Feature the spatial pattern and the theoretical logic.
  • Use the divergence analysis to strengthen construct validity.
  • Keep the wording careful and transparent about limits.
