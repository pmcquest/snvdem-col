Big picture:
- We introduce a method to measure subnational democracy based on V-Dem data.
-- Existing measures tend to focus on observational data, not expert ratings. 
-- V-Dem survey data provides insight into the variables that experts agree are relevant for subnational democracy.
-- We use observational data for these variables to estimate the relevance of these factors across municipalities.
- Our measurement approach uses a data reduction technique (factor analysis) that provides municipal-level weights for (national-level) V-Dem scores. 
- Our pilot study is of Colombia for 2018, but we intend to expand to panel data and other countries.
